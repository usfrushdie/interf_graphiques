WolfWhisper : What is it about ?


WolfWhisper was created, in 2023, by a group of 1st year computer science and networks students at ENSISA supervised by Mr. DEVANNE Maxime and Mr. WEBER Jonathan during a web project.


It is simply a chat application that allows you to chat with people in a modest and friendly atmosphere. It's easy to use and, don't worry, it's not addictive.


So, join us and have fun with your friends!
